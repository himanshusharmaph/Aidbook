What is Aid Book ?

Aidbook is a platform that bridges the gap between students. It connects top students from colleges and universities all over the world with prospective students seeking admissions.

Founding Story

This platform is built as a result of a very personal experience that our founder had during his entrance while prepping up to get admission to a particular college. He was hoping to have better insights about college and other related things. And so, he tried to seek help from a student studying there but it was a futile attempt. And this is how Aidbook was born.
